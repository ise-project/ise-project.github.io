Basegroup II

Clarity about what is expected out of projects, Project plan

What is expected out of the project is not very clear at the start. The collaboration with the OT students is difficult because both parts have different needs and goals. The final goal is to design a complete smart environment solution which could be technically implemented and answers to some of the requirements. In the real world, we would have to make user testing early on, which is not possible for us. The expectations of the project plan are to plan what, when and how to design that solution.
